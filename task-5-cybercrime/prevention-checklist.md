# Stay Safe Online Checklist for College Students.

-**Use this checklist to protect yourself from cyber threats like phishing, scams, and data breaches. Each tip is actionable—tick them off as you implement.

Scan for malware: Run weekly scans with free tools like Malwarebytes; delete suspicious apps immediately.

Backup data safely: Use encrypted cloud storage like Google Drive with 2FA; avoid untrusted USBs from friends.

Log out everywhere: Always log out of shared college computers or apps after use; check "active sessions" in account settings.

Report incidents fast: For cybercrimes like fraud or harassment, visit National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930 immediately—save these contacts now.

Verify recipient name, amount, and QR code before paying; set daily PIN limits (e.g., ₹5,000) in apps like PhonePe/GPay.

Never share UPI PIN; change it monthly and avoid screenshots or notes.

Spot phishing emails/SMS: Ignore unsolicited messages asking for OTPs, links, or personal info; verify sender by calling official numbers.

Use National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or Helpline 1930 for incidents.
